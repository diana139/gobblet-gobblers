Gobblet-Gobblers - Game
---
A strategic "Stacking Tic-Tac-Toe" game built in C++ using the WinBGIm (graphics.h) library

Players can place pieces on top of smaller ones, adding a layer of memory and strategy to the classic 3x3 grid

Features
---
- Two Modes: Singleplayer (vs AI) and Local Multiplayer.

- Stacking Logic: Use 3 different piece sizes to "gobble" opponents.

- Interactive UI: Mouse-controlled gameplay with sound effects and custom graphics.

- Leaderboard: Automatically logs winners to castigatori.txt.


How to Play
---
- Goal: Get three pieces of your color in a row.

- Moves: Place a new piece or move one already on the board.

- Stacking: You can only place a piece on an empty square or over a smaller piece.
